Event Description

It is a short film contest that will be conducted during Excel 2019, the techno-managerial fest of Govt. Model Engineering College, Thrikkakara. The contest aims at bringing out talented artists who are passionate about cinema and giving them a chance to showcase their work in front of an esteemed panel of filmmakers.

Rules

  • It is an open event. Entries are invited from all.
  • The entries have to be original. Content copied from any external source can lead to direct disqualification.
  • Entry fee is Rs. 200 per team which can be sent through Townscript while registering.
  • The film cannot exceed a time duration of 25 minutes (Including title card and credits).
  • Entries can be in any language but suitable subtitles must be provided for languages other than Malayalam.
  • There is no subject/theme for the competition.
  • The submissions must be in the form of a Google Drive link or a YouTube link and must be submitted during registration.
  • The entries must be sent along with a filled registration form and a copy of the ID proof of one of the crew members (preferably director, cinematographer or actor).
  • Last date for sending entries is 4th, November 2019.
  • If the film has already been released, a YouTube link of the video should be sent to the coordinators of the event.

Event format

  • From all the entries, 10 films will be shortlisted by the panel.
  • The top 10 films will be played on the day of the event in front of the panel of filmmakers who will then decide the winners.
  • Along with the screening, there will be an interactive session where the makers can talk to the audience about their film and their journey as a team.
  • The Best Film will be awarded a cash prize of Rs 5000/- along with a certificate.
  • The second Best Film will be given a cash prize of Rs 3000/- along with a certificate.
  • The third Best Film will be given a cash prize of Rs 2000/- along with a certificate.
  • Online certificates will be awarded to all the shortlisted entries.
