Third Eye Photography

Excel 2019

  • Rules

Event Description

An online photography contest spanning 4 weeks, where participants submit their photographs related to each theme. Each week will feature a unique and interesting theme based on which entries are accepted.

Submit your entries to thirdeye@excelmec.org

Rules

  • A maximum of 2 photos for each theme can be submitted by a participant.
  • All participants should be undergraduate students studying in India.
  • All the entries will be uploaded on Excel MEC Facebook page(https://www.facebook.com/excelmec) with the caption given by the participant and ID provided by the coordinating team under the album Third-Eye Photography 2019 and on the Excel MEC Instagram page(https://www.instagram.com/excelmec) with the hashtags provided by the participant.
  • Winners will be selected
      1.Judges Choice
      2.Peoples Choice
  • Winning Criteria:
      People’s Choice:
        1.Facebook Like - 1Pt
        2.Facebook Comment - 2Pt
        3.Facebook Share - 4Pt
        4.Instagram Like - 2Pt
        5.Instagram Comment - 3Pt
      Judges Choice:
        1.Alignment with the theme - 40%
        2.Creativity - 30%
        3.Photograph Quality - 30%
  • Judge – Saahil Rahman (https://www.instagram.com/saahilrahman).
  • All entries should be submitted in digital format to the event mail ID.
  • All entries should contain a short description of the photo, the name of the participant, the name of the college/organization/school, phone number and, if possible, the exif details of the photo.
  • All entries should be of the format JPEG or JPG and less than 5MB in size.
  • Photos that portray or otherwise include inappropriate and/or offensive content, including provocative nudity, violence, human rights and/or environmental violation, and/or any other content deemed to be contrary to the law, religious, cultural and moral traditions and practices of India, are strictly prohibited and will be immediately disqualified.
  • Watermarks of any kind are NOT allowed. Submitted pictures will be watermarked from our side to avoid misuse of the submitted picture.
  • Any use of unfair means in the form of plagiarism or show of popularity using unfair mechanisms will be disqualified.
  • Excel 2019 reserves the right to call for original JPEG or RAW files with unchanged EXIF for the purpose of authentication. An image may be disregarded if this information cannot be provided.
  • Submitted photos must be original, created and/or taken by the contestant. It must not contain any materials owned or controlled by a third party for which you have not obtained a license, must not infringe the copyright, trademark, moral rights, rights of privacy/publicity or intellectual property rights of any person or entity.

Terms And Conditions

  • The decision of the Excel 2019 judging panel is final and binding for all participants with respect to all matters relating to the contest.
  • This is a skill-based contest and it plays a part in the determination of winners.By submitting your entry, you acknowledge and agree to the rules and regulations of the contest.
  • Rules are subject to change at any point in time.
